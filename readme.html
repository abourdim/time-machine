📖How to Use

Open timemachine.html in any modern browser. Select your language, pick a theme, choose your numeral format, and tap Start Journey. Complete levels in order or toggle Free Play to explore freely. Don't forget the Daily Challenge on the Home screen!

On mobile, tap your browser's share button → "Add to Home Screen" for a native app experience with haptic feedback and swipe navigation.
